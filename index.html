Long Live The Mamba

Kobe Bryant

“There's nobody in this league that can guard him.” -NBA Coach Byron Scott

Here's a timeline of Kobe Bryant's Life

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia to Joe Bryant and Pamela Cox Bryant
  • 1984 - Moves to Italy for father’s basketball career
  • 1996 - Drafted by the Charlotte Hornets and traded to the Los Angeles Lakers
  • 1997 - Wins first Slam Dunk Contest
  • 1998 - Named to first All-Star team
  • 2000-02 - Wins three consecutive NBA Championships with Shaquille O’Neal
  • 2008 - Wins first and only league MVP award
  • 2009-10 - Wins two more Championships with Pau Gasol
  • 2012 - Alongside LeBron James and Kevin Durant, wins a second gold in London at 2012 Olympic Games.
  • 2014 - Passes Michael Jordan for third all-time leading scorer. Currently sits at 4th, behind LBJ.
  • 2016 - Retires after 20 seasons with Lakers. Scores 60 points in final game
  • 2018 - Awarded an Oscar for Best Animated Short Film (Dear Basketball)
  • 2020 - Dies in helicopter with daughter Gianna and seven others, just outside Los Angeles.

“When we are saying this cannot be accomplished, this cannot be done, then we are short-changing ourselves. My brain, it cannot process failure. It will not process failure. Because if I have to sit there and face myself and tell myself ‘you are a failure,’ I think that is almost worse than dying.” - Kobe
